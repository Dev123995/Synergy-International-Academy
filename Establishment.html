Establishment of Sri Prakash Synergy School

WHAT YEAR DID SRI PRAKASH SYNERGY ESTABLISHED?

A.Sri Prakash was established on 1977 by Sri Ch. V.K. Narasimha Rao.Some Schools are:-

Sri Prakash Vidya Niketan, Tuni
The first school in the group, established in 1977 with 112 students. It was the first English medium school in the town.

Sri Prakash Vidya Niketan, Payakaraopeta
in 2005, this school is affiliated with the CBSE and has over 2,500 students

Sri Prakash Vidyaniketan, Visakhapatnam
in 2001, this school offers primary education in English using the CBSE and State Board curriculum.

Sri Prakash Synergy School, Kakinada
Established in 2014

Sri Prakash Vidya Niketan, Annavaram
in 2015, this school is located in Mythri Township, Annavaram X roads, Bendapudi Village, Thondangi Mandal, East Godavari District.
